When it comes to fishing, you never get to decide how big the fish you catch is. That's why we invented cameras, so that we can make them look bigger. Fishing is fun because it is almost like a game, you always try to find a new way to catch the biggest fish, especially before anyone else on the boat does. The only bad part is the times when you don't catch any fish, and the worst part is when the fish you catch aren't very big at all.

The main place that my family and I go is the Mississippi river, where there are plenty of spots to catch plenty of fish.
